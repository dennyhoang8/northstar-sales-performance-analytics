# NorthStar Sales Performance Analytics

## Project Overview

This project simulates a real-world business analytics engagement for **NorthStar Outfitters**, a fictional retail company specializing in outdoor equipment, apparel, and sporting goods across the United States.

Using Python, SQL, Power BI, Tableau, and modern data analytics techniques, this project transforms raw sales transaction data into actionable business insights through data cleaning, exploratory analysis, KPI reporting, interactive dashboards, and executive-level recommendations.

The objective is to demonstrate an end-to-end analytics workflow that mirrors how data analysts solve real business problems and communicate findings to stakeholders.

---

## Business Questions

This project answers the following business questions:

- What is the company's total sales revenue?
- Which products generate the highest revenue?
- Which products generate the highest profit?
- Which product categories underperform?
- Which regions and states perform the best?
- Who are the highest-value customers?
- How do discounts impact profitability?
- What sales trends exist over time?
- Which customer segments generate the greatest business value?
- What recommendations can improve overall profitability?
