# Business Requirements Document (BRD)

# NorthStar Outfitters
## Enterprise Sales & Business Analytics Platform

---

## 1. Project Overview

NorthStar Outfitters is a fictional retail company specializing in outdoor equipment, apparel, and sporting goods across the United States.

The executive leadership team has requested a comprehensive analysis of company sales performance to better understand revenue trends, profitability, customer behavior, and regional performance.

This project simulates a real-world consulting engagement using a publicly available retail sales dataset.

---

## 2. Company Background

NorthStar Outfitters operates multiple retail locations and online sales channels throughout the United States.

Although revenue has steadily increased over recent years, leadership has observed inconsistent profit growth and limited visibility into the factors driving business performance.

The company requires a centralized analytics solution capable of transforming raw sales data into actionable business insights.

---

## 3. Business Problem

Executive leadership lacks visibility into the company's overall sales performance.

Current reporting is fragmented across departments, making it difficult to identify:

- High-performing products
- Underperforming regions
- Customer purchasing behavior
- Profitability trends
- Sales opportunities

Leadership requires data-driven recommendations to improve profitability and support strategic decision-making.

---

## 4. Project Objectives

The objectives of this project are to:

- Analyze historical sales performance
- Identify key revenue and profit drivers
- Evaluate customer purchasing behavior
- Analyze regional and product performance
- Develop executive dashboards
- Provide actionable business recommendations

---

## 5. Project Scope

This project includes:

- Data cleaning
- Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)
- SQL analysis
- Statistical analysis
- Data visualization
- KPI development
- Power BI dashboard
- Tableau dashboard
- Executive reporting

---

## 6. Key Business Questions

The analysis will answer the following questions:

1. Which products generate the highest revenue?

2. Which products generate the highest profit?

3. Which product categories underperform?

4. Which regions are most profitable?

5. Which states produce the highest sales?

6. Who are the company's highest-value customers?

7. How do discounts impact profitability?

8. What sales trends exist over time?

9. Which customer segments generate the greatest business value?

10. What recommendations can improve profitability?

---

## 7. Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

Primary KPIs include:

- Total Sales
- Total Profit
- Profit Margin
- Average Order Value
- Sales Growth
- Profit Growth
- Quantity Sold
- Customer Lifetime Value (Estimated)
- Regional Performance
- Category Performance

---

## 8. Deliverables

The final project will include:

- Python analytics workflow
- SQL business queries
- Statistical analysis
- Interactive visualizations
- Power BI dashboard
- Tableau dashboard
- Executive report
- Business recommendations
- GitHub repository

---

## 9. Success Criteria

The project will be considered successful if leadership can:

- Understand overall business performance
- Identify opportunities to increase profitability
- Improve decision-making through data
- Monitor KPIs using interactive dashboards
- Implement recommendations based on analytical findings
